Welcome to the Dianthe Project!

( pronounced dye / ANN / thee )

Our mission is to provide marginalized single mothers with the resources, training, and support they need to pursue careers in the tech industry.

We believe that by empowering women to acquire in-demand skills and secure stable, well-paying jobs, we can help them build a brighter future for themselves and their families.

We will be opening our doors to moms in Atlanta soon!
