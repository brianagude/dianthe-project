Welcome to the Dianthe Project!

( pronounced dye / ANN / thee )

Our mission is to provide single mothers that are living on the margins with the resources, training, and support they need to pursue careers in the tech industry.

We believe that by empowering these women to acquire in-demand skills and secure stable, well-paying jobs, we can help them build a brighter future for themselves and their families.

We will be opening our doors to moms in Atlanta soon!
